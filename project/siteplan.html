Telecommunications Technology and Networks

Telecommunications technology and networks are the backbone of modern communication, enabling the exchange of information across the globe. This field encompasses a wide range of technologies, including wired and wireless communication systems, satellite communication, fiber optics, and the internet. Telecommunications networks facilitate voice calls, video conferencing, data transfer, and access to online services, making them essential for both personal and business communication in today's interconnected world.
